Before Order:

1. All registered users earn points/cashback via

a) Shopping: Every time you shop on our website, 7.5% of total order amount is added as cashback in your ComicSense wallet once the order is delivered.

b) Reviews: For every approved review added by you to the product, Rs. 5 (max Rs. 25 per month) is added into your ComicSense wallet.

2. You can still use the points from you wallet while placing your order during checkout.

3. You can check the balance in your wallet here: ComicSense Wallet.

4. Points in your wallet expire in 100 days from the day they were added.
